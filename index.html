Why even bother with becoming an Marketing Affiliate? Affiliate marketing can be very lucrative. Essentially affiliate marketing involves a merchant paying a commission to other online entities, known as affiliates, for referring new business to the merchant's website. Affiliate marketing is performance-based, which means affiliates only get paid when their promotional efforts actually result in a transaction. So it is vital you drive up prospects by creating a blog/review style site. Customers appreciate honest reviews and so does your wallet!
